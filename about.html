About the CSE Department

Excellence in Education, Innovation & Research at Barishal University

Overview

Established in 2014, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Barishal University is the first and only engineering department in the university. The department offers a comprehensive BSc in CSE program, designed to provide students with strong theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing them for careers in academia, research, and the IT industry.

Mission & Vision

To provide high-quality education in computer science and engineering, fostering innovation and research that addresses real-world challenges.
To become a leading center of excellence in computer science and engineering education and research, contributing significantly to technological advancements and societal development.
